Get It On Android

Epic Grind isn't on the Play Store yet. You'll install it directly from the APK file — a five-minute setup done once on your device.

Install Steps
1
Allow installs from unknown sources

Android blocks apps from outside the Play Store by default. Go to Settings → Apps → Special app access → Install unknown apps, then find your browser (Chrome or Firefox) and toggle it on.

Path varies by device — search "install unknown apps" in Settings if you can't find it.
2
Open this page on your phone

The download needs to land directly on your Android device. If you're reading this on a computer, open epicgrind.app/android in your phone's browser or scan the QR code below when it's available.

3
Download the APK

Tap the download button below. The file is roughly 20–40 MB. Your browser will download it to your device's Downloads folder.

epic-grind-alpha.apk
Build r10 · 2026-07-04 · v1.0.0+10
4
Run the installer

Open your Downloads folder and tap the APK file. Android will ask you to confirm the install — tap Install. The app appears on your home screen when it's done.

5
Launch Epic Grind

Tap the app icon. Create an account or sign in. Orin will take it from there.

↻
Updating future builds

Alpha builds update frequently. When a new APK ships, repeat steps 3–4. Your account and progress carry over automatically.

Troubleshooting
?
"App not installed" error

Usually means an older version of the APK is still on the device. Go to Settings → Apps → Epic Grind and uninstall it first, then retry. Your account data lives on the server — it won't be lost.

?
Google Play Protect warning

Play Protect may flag unsigned apps. Tap Install anyway — the warning is expected for builds outside the Play Store. Epic Grind does not collect data beyond what's required for your account.
