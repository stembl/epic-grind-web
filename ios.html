iOS · TestFlight Install

Get It On iPhone

iOS testing runs through Apple's internal TestFlight. There is no public link — Steve adds testers manually by Apple ID. Send him your Apple ID email and he'll get you in.

✓ Build Live — Invite Only

The TestFlight build is active. Access is internal — Steve adds testers one at a time by Apple ID. Send him your Apple ID email (the one linked to your iPhone) and he'll add you within 24 hours.

Install Steps
1
Install TestFlight

TestFlight is Apple's official beta testing app. It's free and required to install pre-release builds on iPhone.

TestFlight on the App Store
Free · Apple Inc.
2
Send Steve your Apple ID email

There is no public TestFlight link. Send Steve the email address tied to your Apple ID — he'll add you manually in App Store Connect. Apple then emails you an invitation directly. Don't forward that invite — it's tied to your account.

Your Apple ID email is found in Settings → [your name] at the top of your iPhone.
3
Open the invite link on your iPhone

Tap the link in the message you receive. It will open in Safari and redirect you into TestFlight. Tap Accept on the invitation.

The link must be opened on the iPhone you plan to test on — not a computer.
4
Install from TestFlight

Inside TestFlight, find Epic Grind and tap Install. The app downloads to your home screen like any App Store app.

TestFlight invite link
Personal invite — sent by Steve once your Apple ID is added
5
Launch Epic Grind

Tap the app icon. Create an account or sign in. Orin will take it from there.

↻
Updates are automatic

TestFlight notifies you when a new build is available. Tap Update inside the TestFlight app. Your account and progress carry over automatically.

Troubleshooting
?
Invite link says "expired" or "full"

TestFlight invites expire after 90 days and are capped at 10,000 testers per build. Contact Steve directly for a fresh link if yours has expired.

?
App crashes on launch

Alpha builds are rough. Force-close and reopen once. If it persists, TestFlight includes a built-in crash reporter — it sends the log automatically. That data goes directly to the dev team and is the fastest path to a fix.

?
Don't have an iPhone?

Android is the primary test platform for early alpha. See the Android install guide — the APK sideload process is simpler and ships first.
